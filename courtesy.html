Making of this website

I always wanted to join community events around Swift. I started my travel journey with Apple Accelerator, where I met Amit Samant, and we connected over LinkedIn. One of Swift Bengaluruâ€™s posts landed on my feed, and I applied to become a speaker. This is where I met Nayyan, Abhishek, and other folks.
One day I got a message from Nayaan about building up this site, and my inner calling to contribute more to the community came alive. Yes, I have found motivation to contribute back to the community. But I needed someone to help me with creating images and choosing a color theme for the website.
Meet my better half, Anjali Saraf, who worked tirelessly (along with her little one finding his way out into the world) to make this vision a reality. After endless battles (yes, we had many over pictures and theme selections), we finalized what you see in this section.
This is nowhere near perfect, and we would love your suggestions to make it better. Please send a message over LinkedIn or email to get in touch if you would like to contribute and get here. Please hit like to motivate both of us to continue doing this.
